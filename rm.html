ABOUT ME - RM

"I Was Lost but God Loved Me."

I wandered through shadows, empty and cold, Chasing the world, its glitter, its gold. My heart was restless, my spirit astray, I stumbled in darkness, far from the way.

But Love unending reached out to my soul, A gentle whisper began to console. Though broken and weary, I heard His call, Grace lifted me up when I thought I would fall.

I was lost, yet He sought me still, Guided my steps with a tender will. No sin too heavy, no night too deep, His mercy found me, His promise to keep.

Now I walk in light, redeemed and free, Held by the One who first loved me. Forever I’ll sing, my heart will proclaim: I was lost, but God loved me the same.
